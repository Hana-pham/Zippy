zippy_screens_detector

Screen extraction from figma file.

Our proposal to leverage innovative capabilities of GPT-4 combined with Python for analysing Figma designs for automated code generation. This project reflects our goal to streamline the design-to-code transition, a critical challenge to designers and developers alike. By integrating advanced AI with appropriate image analysis, we aim to offer a solution that not only accelerates design workflows but also enhances the fidelity of the final output.
